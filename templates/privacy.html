Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, government identifiers, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the expense tracking service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure and hosting. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are only given access to your data as necessary to perform their functions. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us. We are committed to responding to privacy requests in a timely manner and working with you to address any concerns.
